Privacy Policy for Clear Mind Notes

Last updated: 01-21-2025 (DD-MM-YYYY)

Thank you for using Clear Mind Notes. Your privacy is very important to us. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data:

No Data Collection

Clear Mind Notes is an offline application. We do not collect, save, or share any user data. All notes written in the app exist temporarily and are cleared upon user request. No information is stored on your device or transmitted to any server.

No Internet Connectivity

The app operates entirely offline. It does not access the internet, ensuring your notes remain private and transient.

No Third-Party Services

Clear Mind Notes does not integrate with any third-party services or tools that collect or process user data.

Data Security

As no data is collected or stored, there are no data security concerns related to Clear Mind Notes.

Children's Privacy

Clear Mind Notes is suitable for users of all ages. As no data is collected, the app complies with child privacy regulations such as COPPA.
